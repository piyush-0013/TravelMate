TRIP TO RAJASTHAN (7 Days & 8 Nights)




Get pampered in luxury within the finest ambience in Rajasthan

Radisson Rajasthan, a 4-star hotel in the beautiful city of Rajasthan, is a perfect blend of luxury and comfort. Located near the Lake City Mall, the hotel is within 5 km from the City Palace, Fateh Sagar Lake and Jagdish Temple.

Hotel Facilities
Radisson Rajasthan offers various services including a rooftop pool, spa, steam room, restaurant and lounge bar. The hotel has a business centre, 6 meeting rooms and free Wi-Fi services. Other services available are on-site parking, concierge services, express laundry, travel services and 24-hour room service.

Room Facilities
The hotel has 52 rooms and 4 suites including Superior Rooms, Business Class Rooms and Executive Suites. They are pleasantly decked and equipped with work desk, seating area, mini refrigerator, LED TV, coffee or tea facilities, 24-hour room service, free Wi-Fi and wood floors. Shower, hair dryer and shaving mirror are also available.

Dining
Radisson Rajasthan has an on-site restaurant, Seasons Cafe which offers various cuisines including Mexican, Chinese and Italian as well as Indian delicacies. You can also relax at the F3 Lounge with some drinks and finger food. Buffet rates as follows: Buffet Breakfast Rate INR 599 + 18% tax/person Buffet Lunch Rate INR 899 + 18% tax/person Buffet Dinner Rate INR 1299 + 18% tax/person

Important Landmarks
Some must-see attractions of the city nearby include Lake City Mall (about 1 km), Gulabh Bagh and Zoo (about 4 km), Fateh Sagar Lake (4 km), City Palace (4 km), Sahelion-ki-Bari (about 4 km), Lake Pichola (less than 5 km) and Ropeway (5 km).

The Rajasthan Railway Station and Rajasthan Bus Station are both about 3 km away whereas the Maharana Pratap Airport is 21 km away.

The hotel is in the heart of the city, close to various banks. Other important business centres include The Asian Business Centre (less than 2 km), Smart City Rajasthan (about 2 km), RTDC Office (3 km), Office of Analyst Cum Programmer DoITC (4 km) and Rajasthan Department of Mines and Geology (6 km). What people love the most about this property is its great location and classy ambience.
