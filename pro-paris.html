INTERNATIONAL DESTINATIONS

TRIP TO PARIS (5 Days & 6 Nights)




DETAILED DAY WISE ITINERARY

Day 1 - Arrival at Paris

• Arrive Paris and meet with our representative at Paris Airport for your private transfer to hotel. Check in and rest of the time free at your own. Overnight at Paris

Day 2 - City Tour with Seine Cruise & Eiffel Tower with Skip the line

• Discover Paris most famous tourist sights and monuments on one unique tour. This tour features a visit to the 2nd floor of the Eiffel Tower with breath taking views of the capital, a panoramic city tour by coach, plus an hour-long boat trip along the Seine. This comprehensive tour is the ideal way to discover Paris for the first time. Your tour by air conditioned panoramic coach offers you the opportunity to see Paris and learn about its history and most famous sights. Audio commentaries will tell you the story of the most significant Parisian districts and monuments, from the Opera Garnier to the Eiffel Tower or Notre-Dame de Paris Cathedral. Among the essential Parisian sites described on the HistoPad, you will not miss the lively districts of the Champs Elysees and Saint Germain des Pres, the musee dOrsay or the Conciergerie where Marie-Antoinette was imprisoned. At the foot of the Eiffel Tower, step aboard an hour-long Seine cruise with an audio guide. Marvel at the fantastic architecture of the heart of Paris most prestigious monuments lining the banks of the Seine.

Day 3 - Disneyland Tour (Optional)

•Enjoy your Parisian breakfast and stay prepared for a thrilling day at Disneyland (Optional - not included in the package cost). We begin our day by crossing Paris as far as the Porte de Bercy to join the A4 motorway leading to Disneyland Paris. Our driver will leave you at the group parking area close to the park entrances. An entertainment resort housing 2 theme parks, hotels, and shopping canters -- Disneyland entertains kids and adults alike. Having gotten in touch with the kids in yourselves, retreat to your hotel and drift away into the world of dreams. At Disneyland Park visit Magic world where you will meet all the Disney characters who live in an endless fairy tale.

Day 4 - Paris - Zurich - Interlaken

•After breakfast check out and transfer to Paris Airport on a private car for your flight to Zurich. On landing in Zurich, you will find yourself transported in a fairy-tale world. From Zurich Airport hop on a train to Interlaken and reach at your hotel. Post-check-in, take your time to shed off the weariness. Once you get refreshed, set off to explore the elegant lanes, glass-like lakes, lively streams, and local markets of this beautiful city. Choose from the numerous fascinating attractions of the city and return for a comfortable overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5 - Visit Mount Titlis

• After a delicious breakfast spend a day at Mt Titlis and get ready to live the precious moments of being at Mt Titlis in person. Feel the chill of a European morning along with your better half. Seat yourself in the first ever revolving gondola to Mt Titlis and leave the rest to the mesmerizing sceneries. From Lucerne, take the LSE train to Engelberg and shift to a small gondola up to the Trubsee. Another gondola would take you up till Stand before finally dropping you to Mt Titlis. Witness the grandeur of the Swiss mountains like never before and set free your curious souls. Visit the glacier cave and the Ice Flyer Chairlift and relish Indian or Continental food at the restaurant on top for a lunch date to remember.
